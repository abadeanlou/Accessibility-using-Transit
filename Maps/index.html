How far can Torino take you?

Interactive maps of public-transport accessibility for Torino, computed from open GTFS schedules: the transit network is rebuilt in MongoDB, travel times are routed over a hexagonal grid, and each cell is colored by how much of the city it can reach. Methodology from my first-author papers at hEART 2022 and TRB 2023.
